History

At the end of November 2020, two funny guys made a funny company called 'Tea Team'. Their names was Bruno Szewczyk and Jakub Namyślak. In the beginning, they were programming in python. Bruno was programming PY-DOS, and Jacob was programming ShatterOS. For a few days, they employed 'sheym1b', Bruno's cousin. But he left from the company few days after he was employed. But Bruno and Jacob weren't sad. They were doing a lot of projects. On February 13th, 'Szczabelik' joined the team, Tea Team. He was making some logos and many different graphic thing for the company. Two months later, Jacob left the team. After that, Tea Team was in the worst condition. But it was OK. Bruno developed many versions of PY - DOS. Bruno and 'Szczabelik' were doing all right. On the May 16th, Piotr Rogowski joined the team. And we are in present. Tea Team is small company that distributes its own software and music. It consists of three ambitious people: Bruno Szewczyk, 'Szczabelik' and Piotr Rogowski. What will happen in the future? We don't know...

Python Modules Hub

PMH (Python Modules Hub) is a application, that runs python scripts and .exe programs

Using PMH, you can save a lot of time, because you don't need to manage multiple windows and directories
