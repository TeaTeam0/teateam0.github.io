But Bruno and Jacob weren't sad. They were doing a lot of projects. On February 13th, 'Szczabelik' joined the team, Tea Team. Bruno developed many versions of PY - DOS. Bruno and 'Szczabelik' were doing all right. On the May 16th, Piotr Rogowski joined the team. And we are in present.
